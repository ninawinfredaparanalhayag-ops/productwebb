Warranty Claim Policy

  1. The following conditions are subject to warranty claims:
    • Moisture
    • Insulation issue (not retaining temperature)
    • Rusting
    • Rattling sound
    • Leaks due to factory defects
  2. Claims can be made within 1 year from the date of purchase.
  3. Original receipt or proof of purchase must be presented when claiming warranty.
