About Us

Introducing EcoMug, a blend of the words Eco-friendly and Mug. “Eco-friendly” means less or no harmful effect on the environment, while a “mug” is a type of cup commonly used for beverages.

Keep your drinks at the perfect temperature with our durable, ergonomically designed EcoMug. Its stainless steel insulation maintains the chill of iced drinks and the warmth of hot drinks for up to 12 hours.

What’s more? Every purchase is secured by a limited 1-year warranty.
