Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the compartment located underneath the mug?

▼
The compartment underneath the mug serves the vital function of allowing users to store small essentials, such as sugar, tea bags, coffee, or snacks.

What is the primary environmental benefit of using the Mugtainer?

▼
The Mugtainer is an eco-friendly product made from biodegradable and recyclable materials. It is designed to reduce the use of single-use plastics and disposable cups, which helps to minimize environmental impact and supports a zero-waste lifestyle.

Does the Mugtainer help keep drinks hot or cold?

▼
Yes, the mug is designed to maintain insulation. The use of stainless steel as an inner lining enhances temperature retention, allowing drinks to stay hot or cold for extended periods.

Is the storage compartment easy to clean and convenient to use?

▼
Yes, the detachable compartment is both convenient to use and easy to clean using sponge or brush.

What are the compartments for?

▼
The separate compartments are designed for storing small accompaniments, such as medicine, coffee sticks, snacks (nuts, seeds, etc.), and tea bags.

Is the Mugtainer leakproof?

▼
Mugtainers are designed with a secure, tight-fitting lid (often with a silicone seal) to be leak-resistant or leakproof for travel. However, always ensure the lid is properly secured before placing it in a bag.

How much liquid does the Mugtainer hold?

▼
Capacity varies by model, but most are typically sized like a standard travel mug, ranging from 12 oz (350 ml) to 16 oz (470 ml).

How long is the warranty period?

▼
The warranty period is a maximum of 1 year.
